About me

My love for photography began when I was 14 years old and my high school offered a photography class as a elective. It took 3 years before I was chosen to take this class. Once I was in this class, the love and joy I felt in capturing people's smiles just warmed my heart. I new this was my calling, what I suppose to do. I have always felt that photography was moments never forgotten, a memory you will always have. Now after 33 years I still feel that same joy and want to share that love with you and your family.

My Experience

During my 33 years of experience in photography I have worked on different projects including: Weddings, graduations, fashion shows, sports events, senior picture, baby shower and more.
